**6. Final Microcontroller Choice**: **PIC18F27Q10**

**Rationale**: The PIC18F27Q10 is the best selection due to its small
footprint which will fit best on our PCB and having the most flash
memory of the three options. The price is reasonable and it checks out
with all the requirements needed for our product.
